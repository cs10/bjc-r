Homework

How do you interact with computers and digital media? Google doc
This initial writing assignment can help you gauge both students' writing abilities and familiarity with computers. This is also their first assignment on Google docs, so you can go over the mechanics of accessing their assignment. This assignment can also be completed offline for those without the necessary access at home.

Prepare

  • Prepare a Computing in the News story.
  • Make sure students know the procedure for doing their homework and turning it in, especially if they will do this assignment online.
