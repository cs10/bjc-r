Unit 1 Teacher Guide

Week 1 (Programming Focus)

During Week 1, students will work on Labs 1-3 of Unit 1.
  • Programming Lab 1: Introduction (student pages) (teacher support) ~ 60 minutes
  • Programming Lab 2: The Stage (student pages) (teacher support) ~ 80 minutes
  • Programming Lab 3: Repeating Commands (student pages) (teacher support) ~ 100 minutes


Week 2 (Programming Focus)

During Week 2, students complete Labs 4-6 of Unit 1.
  • Programming Lab 4: Making Your Own Blocks (student pages) (teacher support) ~ 60 minutes
  • Programming Lab 5: Drawing Complicated Blocks (student pages) (teacher support) ~ 90 minutes
  • Programming Lab 6: Using Randomness (student pages) (teacher support) ~ 90 minutes
