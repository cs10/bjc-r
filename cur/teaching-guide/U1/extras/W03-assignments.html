Week 3 Assignments

Optional assignments for class discussion, silent work, or homework:

  • Read the article Demystifying the Algorithm: Who Designs Your Life?
    • This article includes several examples of technologies that might affect your life in unexpected, unintended, or surprising ways. What are some of these examples? What are other examples?
    • This article also talks about the need for people to understand and take responsibility for potentially harmful effects of the technology they develop. What are some of the challenges?
    • The story of the motorist trapped in a traffic circle is a made up story. Write your own short, made-up article about a technology innovation gone wrong.
