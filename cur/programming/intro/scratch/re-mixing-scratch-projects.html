"Remixing" is the Scratch communities term for downloading another user's project, enhancing it in some way, and then re-posting it. Scratch community members see re-mixing as a complement and earn notoriety for the number of re-mixes their projects inspire.

In this activity, download someone else's project and make a change. You may decide to re-post the project or not.

If you are interested in exploring sound and music more, download Alex Ruthmann's, of the University of Massachusetts Lowell, amazing projects here. His techniques are advanced but you'll be able to play with what he has done and see what can Scratch offers.
