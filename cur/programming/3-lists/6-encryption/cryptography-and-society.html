  1. The following are prompts from the AP Explore Task. Write about your innovation, responding to each prompt in a separate paragraph. Remember to cite your sources.
    1. the innovation name and a description of the intended purpose of the innovation (100 words max)
    2. an explanation of the technical details of this innovation in terms that someone completely unfamiliar with the innovation would understand (100 words max)
    3. a description of the role computing plays in implementing the functionality associated with the innovation (100 words max)
    4. a description of the relationship between data and the innvoation. For example, you could describe the data used or produced by the innovation or any privacy issues associated with the innovation data (100 words max).
  2. Trade your paragraphs with a partner. Read their response for each prompt. Did they write about what the prompts asked for?
