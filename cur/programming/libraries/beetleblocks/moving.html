Now you will try moving the sprite around in 3 dimensions. In the Motion tab, you will notice that the blocks are nearly identical to Snap! blocks. Every block that requires an axis input has options for x, y, and z axes. It should also be mentioned here that there is one rotate block rather than the two turn clockwise/counterclockwise blocks in Snap! The rotate block functions more similarly to the turn counterclockwise block. Try out the blocks to familiarize yourself with them and how they work.

In the Shapes tab, the and the blocks function in the same way as the pen up and pen down block in Snap!
